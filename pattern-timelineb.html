How the belief formed

A pattern isn't an objective fact about what happened, it's a set of experiences your younger self interpreted the same way, with different people, at different points in your life. A belief is the conclusion your nervous system reached after those experiences seemed to keep confirming the same thing. Once that conclusion forms, it starts shaping how later moments get read too, so the pattern can keep going even when a later event isn't as clear cut as the first ones.
