02 — The Pattern
Where You
Created Distance
This isn't a blame exercise — distance made sense once. The question is just: where did it show up this week, and what was underneath it?
SPACE
A
The distance
Where did you pull back this week?
Not as a criticism — just as a noticing. The DA system creates distance in many forms. Some obvious, some subtle.
Did you create distance from someone this week?
What form did the distance take?
Did the other person notice? How do you know?
B
What was underneath it
What was the distance protecting you from?
The DA system creates distance as protection — not as coldness. What was it guarding against this week?
What would have happened if you hadn't created the distance?
Was the threat real this week — or was it a familiar feeling from somewhere older?
What does the distance cost you — if anything?
C
The honest question
Is the distance still serving you?
No right answer. Just an honest one.
When you look at the distance you created this week — does it feel like protection, or like something you want less of?
